<div align="center">
  <h2>What is Solvedge?</h2>
</div>

<br>

**Solvedge** is a sleek analytics dashboard for competitive programmers on [Codeforces](https://codeforces.com). Enter any handle and instantly get data-driven insights into your **strengths**, **weaknesses**, and **blind spots** — all powered by statistical analysis of your real submission history.

No guesswork. No generic advice. Just your data, decoded.

<br><br>

---

<br>

<div align="center">
  <h2>Features</h2>
</div>

<br>

<div align="center">

| | |
|:---|:---|
| **Acceptance Rate Analysis** | Tag-by-tag breakdown with real solve rates |
| **Untouched Topics** | High-frequency tags at your level you haven't tried |
| **Weak Area Detection** | Automatically surfaces your weakest tags |
| **Solve Streak Tracking** | Keep your daily motivation sharp |
| **365-Day Heatmap** | GitHub-style activity map of your solves |
| **Recommendation Engine** | AI-powered problem suggestions tailored to your profile |

</div>
